Purpose of this project: 

Motivation is a big part of my daily routine. Having that small paragraph that reminds me to 
keep pushing myself to achieve what I want is something I needed. My usual place to go to get some
motivation is tiktok motivational pages or spotify podcasts or youtube videos. So I decided to 
create a website that allows me to store the motivational paragraphs that I've fallen in love with
or that helped me along in my journey through life.
